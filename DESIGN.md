# YUYA Template Design System (Cloned)

## Foundation
- Minimalist, high contrast (black/white).
- Typography-driven layout.

## Colors
- Background: `#FFFFFF` (White)
- Text: `#000000` (Black)

## Typography
- Font Family: `Inter`, `sans-serif`
- H1: 174px, 600 weight
- H4: 48px, 500 weight
- Body/Nav: 12px, 400 weight
